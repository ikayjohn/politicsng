The mandate

Representation that stays close to the people and delivers measurable results.

Ciroma Chukwuma Adekunle is running to represent the Federal Capital Territory with a Senate office that listens across Abuja Municipal, Bwari, Gwagwalada, Kuje, Kwali, and Abaji.

Core agenda

Four priorities for a stronger constituency.

01

Jobs and enterprise

Back Abuja's traders, artisans, tech workers, and young entrepreneurs with skills, credit access, and market infrastructure.

02

Community security

Strengthen lawful community policing, emergency response, lighting, and local intelligence partnerships across the FCT.

03

Education and skills

Expand scholarships, technical colleges, digital literacy, teacher support, and school improvement partnerships.

04

Transparent service

Publish Abuja constituency updates, project tracking, open town halls, and regular area council office hours.

How he will serve

A Senate office built around access, evidence, and follow-through.

  • Monthly constituency briefings Clear reports on motions, bills, interventions, and federal projects affecting Abuja residents.
  • Area council contact network Local coordinators in all six area councils who keep the office connected to real issues on the ground.
  • Issue-based legislation Policy work shaped by data on security, infrastructure, education, healthcare, housing, and enterprise.
